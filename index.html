About Me

I'm a software developer based in Kansas City!

I decided to start my professional career by learning marketing strategies and developing online presence for small to medium sized businesses. As a result of that, I had the opportunity to work with countless businesses across different industries and become familiar with the unique needs and tactics to help their business flourish. I discovered my passion for helping businesses get found, get chosen and retain customers.


I transitioned into software engineering to better understand how the applications and software we use in our daily lives are designed and implemented around ideal user experiences. I incorporate my previous marketing and customer service experience along with my software engineering skills to provide creative, efficient, and customized solutions.


When I'm not coding I enjoy spending time with my family, camping, listening to music and reading.
